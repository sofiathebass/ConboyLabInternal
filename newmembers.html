Gloves

General Rules about gloves:

  • 1. One Glove Policy
  • 2. No Gloves in the Breakroom
  • 3. Reusing Gloves

1. The one glove policy basically means that you shouldn't be touching doorknobs with gloves. When you're going out of lab to grab something, you can have one glove on to hold your things, but at least one hand must be ungloved for doors, even if you didn't touch anything super deadly! Say I used gloves to handle, idk, nuclear waste, and touched a completely inert PBS bottle. I put the PBS bottle back, and then you come along and just grab the PBS to use, then put it back and go touch an elevator button. Now this elevator button has nuclear waste, and you didn't even realize it! Even if you didn't touch anything at all with the glove, it's a good habit to always use the one glove policy.
